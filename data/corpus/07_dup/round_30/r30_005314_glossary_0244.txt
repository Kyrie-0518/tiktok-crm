---

## SPU

Standard Product Unit - Parent grouping of related SKUs.

---

## SKU

Stock Keeping Unit - Smallest saleable unit of a product.

---

## CTR

Click-Through Rate - (Clicks / Impressions) x 100%.

---

## CPM

Cost Per Mille - (Total spend / Impressions) x 1000.

---

Enterprise Resource Planning - Integrated business management system.

---

## ROAS

Return On Ad Spend - Ad Revenue / Ad Spend.

---

## KOL

Goods and Services Tax - Common in Southeast Asian markets.

---

## FBA

Fulfillment by Amazon - Amazon's warehouse and shipping service.

---

## GMV

Gross Merchandise Volume - Total transaction value on a platform within a time period.

---

## CPC

Cost Per Click - Total spend / Clicks.

---

## CAC

Customer Acquisition Cost - Total marketing spend / New customers.

---

## ROI

Return On Investment - (Revenue - Cost) / Cost x 100%.

---

## UGC

User Generated Content - reviews, videos, photos by customers.

---

## NPS

Net Promoter Score - Customer loyalty metric, range -100 to +100.

---

## DTC

Direct-to-Consumer - Brands selling directly without intermediaries.

---

## AOV

Average Order Value - Total GMV / Total Orders.
